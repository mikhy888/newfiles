Our Team
Co-Founder & CEO

Trivikraman Thampy

“Show me a person who never made a mistake, and I will show you a person who never did anything.”

The true passion for online games led Trivikraman, fondly called Vikram, to co-establish Games24x7 in 2006. He serves as its CEO, and through his focus on user experience has guided the company through numerous successful product releases, marketing campaigns and rounds of funding.
Co-Founder & CEO

Bhavin Pandya

“Make them feel respected, empowered and genuinely respected about the company’s mission.”

Co-Founder and CEO - In 2006, Bhavin saw the potential in bringing online games to a nascent Indian market and established the business in Mumbai. Bhavin has successfully led the team from the launch of RummyCircle online in 2008 to the launch of RummyCircle on smartphones in 2014.
